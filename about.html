Our Story

GBV Safe Corner was created to provide a safe, anonymous space for anyone affected by gender-based violence. We believe everyone deserves access to support, resources, and community without fear or judgment.

Our Mission

To create a trusted platform where survivors can find help, share experiences, and access resources in a safe, anonymous environment. We aim to break the silence around GBV and build supportive communities for healing and empowerment.

What We Offer

Anonymous Reporting System - Safe, confidential incident reporting with complete privacy protection

Verified Support Directory - Trusted organizations and professionals vetted for your safety

Community Story Sharing - Safe spaces to share experiences and find peer support

Educational Resources - Comprehensive information about GBV prevention, rights, and healing

Event Organization Tools - Platform to mobilize awareness campaigns and support events

Peer Support Community - Anonymous Q&A and community advice sharing

Our Values

Safety First: Your privacy and security are our highest priority. We never collect identifying information.

Trust & Verification: All support organizations are carefully verified for your protection.

Community Support: Building supportive networks and breaking the isolation that GBV creates.

Accessibility: Free and available to everyone, with 24/7 access to critical resources.

Empowerment: Providing tools and information to help survivors regain control and agency.

Get Involved

GBV Safe Corner is built for the community, by the community. You can:

• Share your story to help others feel less alone

• Organize awareness events in your area

• Refer verified support organizations to be listed

• Help spread the word about this safe resource

Together, we can create a safer, more supportive world for everyone affected by gender-based violence.
