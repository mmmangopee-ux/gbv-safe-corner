Our Story

UFHGBVFIGHT was created to address the critical need for safe, anonymous reporting and support systems for gender-based violence on campuses and in communities.

We recognized that many survivors hesitate to report incidents due to fear, stigma, or concerns about privacy. Our platform bridges this gap by providing:
