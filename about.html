Our Story

GBV Safe Corner was created to provide a safe, anonymous space for anyone affected by gender-based violence. We believe everyone deserves access to support, resources, and community.

Our Mission

To create a trusted platform where survivors can find help, share experiences, and access resources without fear or judgment.

What We Offer

• Anonymous reporting system

• Verified support organizations

• Community story sharing

• Educational resources

• Event organization tools

• Peer support community

Our Values

• Safety First: Your privacy and security are our priority

• Trust: All organizations are verified for your protection

• Community: Building supportive networks for healing

• Accessibility: Free and available to everyone
