About Our Mission

Our Story

GBV Safe Corner was born from a critical need for safe, accessible, and anonymous support for survivors of gender-based violence in South Africa. Founded in 2023, our platform emerged from community conversations with survivors, support workers, and advocates who identified gaps in existing support systems.

Our Mission

To create a trusted, anonymous digital sanctuary where survivors of gender-based violence can safely access support, share experiences, and find community without fear of judgment, stigma, or identification. We believe everyone deserves access to help and healing on their own terms.

Our Values

Safety First: Your privacy and security are our highest priority. We never collect identifying information and all data is protected with enterprise-grade security.

Trust & Verification: Every support organization listed undergoes rigorous verification to ensure they meet our standards for safety and professionalism.

Community Support: We believe in the power of shared experiences and building supportive networks that break the isolation GBV creates.

Accessibility: Free and available to everyone, with 24/7 access to critical resources and multi-language support.

Empowerment: Providing tools, information, and community to help survivors regain control, agency, and hope.

Survivor-Centered: Every feature is designed with and for survivors, ensuring our platform meets real needs in safe, practical ways.
